Service FAQ

Clear answers before booking.

A smoother service experience starts with clarity around pricing, appointment type, and private service availability.

FAQ 1

Why is pricing confirmed later?

Final pricing can vary based on service detail, barber selection, add-ons, private setup, or consultation needs.

FAQ 2

Can I request a private appointment?

Yes. Private hijabi and consultation-based services can be requested through the booking flow or direct contact.

FAQ 3

Do both locations offer the same services?

Core barbering services are available across the brand, while some specialty services may depend on barber, setup, or availability.

FAQ 4

How should I choose the right booking option?

Start by selecting the shop and barber that fit best, then choose the service category that matches your appointment goals.
